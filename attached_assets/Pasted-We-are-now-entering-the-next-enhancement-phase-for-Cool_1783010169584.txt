====================================================
PHASE OBJECTIVE
====================================================

Enhance CoolDesk into a premium Business Operating System for HVAC and Service Companies.

The focus of this phase is platform maturity, administration, business workflow, executive visibility, and enterprise usability.

====================================================
1. PRODUCT POSITIONING
====================================================

Position CoolDesk as:

"Business Operating System for HVAC & Service Companies"

instead of simply calling it an ERP.

Update branding where appropriate without affecting business logic.

====================================================
GENERAL RULES
====================================================

Before implementing anything:

1. Analyze existing implementation.
2. Determine if similar functionality already exists.
3. Reuse existing code whenever possible.
4. Never duplicate modules.
5. Keep UI consistent with the CoolDesk design system.
6. Do not break APIs.
7. Do not change business logic unnecessarily.
8. Implement incrementally.
9. Make every feature modular and reusable.
10. Follow enterprise SaaS best practices.

The final implementation should feel like a polished commercial SaaS platform that businesses would confidently subscribe to and use daily.
